By The             Cloud
Safeguard is a portable all-in-one purpose device made for cyclists to aid in collision prevention. Your SafeGuard device is placed on the back and/or front of your bicycle and uses a variety of high precision sensors to assess the surrounding environment, relaying all the information straight back to you on your smartphone and giving you 360Â° safety
What is SafeGuard?
The SafeGuard device uses four high-quality pieces of hardware.An Ultrasonic range finder, an accelerometer, RGB LED and Piezo buzzer. Our high precision Ultrasonic rangefinder and accelerometer sensor are used in a variety of ways; it detects the proximity of objects behind you through the detection of ultrasonic wave echoes, performs collision prediction, determines your speed/acceleration to determine instances of collision. All information is relayed in real time to your dominate senses using an RGB LED and Piezo buzzer at the front of your bicycle. To make the best use of your SafeGuard Device, strap your smartphone to the front of the bicycle and while being connected to SafeGuard, you can view speed, acceleration, navigation and several other features in real-time!

Plan Ahead
When you cycle you only ever see what's in front of you. You are oblivious to what is happening behind you. By attaching the SafeGuard device on the rear of your bicycle you will have a near 360Â° awareness of your surroundings. The SafeGuard device will notify you how close objects are around you through visual feedback such as your smartphone or an RGB LED light which is positioned at the front of your bicycle which changes color from red, amber, green depending on object proximity. Objects too close will automatically trigger a piezo buzzer notifying you of an imminent collision so that you can keep safe on the roads

We are all cyclist enthusiasts and have always found it difficult at times to fully be aware of our surroundings, especially in metropolitan areas. Most of us have personally been involved in a collision in one form or another, but usually from behind. How could we stop this? Our search for the answer led us all the way here. We noticed that as we ride on a cycle we only ever see what's in front of us. Just a measly 135-degree field of view leaving behind 225 degrees of uncertainty. Yet, we couldn't find anything that could help us and other cyclists be safe, so we took on the issue ourselves
Our Story
Inspired by the prospect of possibly increasing cyclist safety, we brainstormed various solutions. We found that SafeGuard is the ultimate solution to increasing awareness of cyclists and ensuring their safety.
